The many faces of The Garage

Being lifelong, professional musicians, The Garage can transform into any type of group you need.

From a full country band to a rockin' wedding band to suit any size venue, let us help you put together the background vibe for your event.

We don't want to say how many years live music experience we have all together, suffice to say that we're the best at what we do.

Duo/Trio

Start your trip down the aisle with a classy duo, then step it up later with the full band.

Do you need a quiet trio for a corporate event? What about a power trio for your company party? The Garage has something to suit your music needs.

Full Band

Want the full band experience for your corporate or block party? We know what you're looking for. Check out our set lists and let us customize your live music experience.

Country Band

The Garage has been playing country hits and classics for more than a decade. Need some event countrification? No problem, we're here for you.
